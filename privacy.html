Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how oqe8.cc (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you visit our website or purchase our digital products.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require users to create accounts and do not intentionally collect or store personal information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, or payment details.

2. Payment Information

All payments are processed by third-party payment providers, including Stripe, PayPal, and Cash App. We do not store, process, or have access to your full payment information.

Payment providers may collect information necessary to process your transaction in accordance with their own privacy policies.

3. Automatically Collected Data

Like most websites, limited technical data may be automatically collected by our hosting provider or third-party services for basic functionality and security purposes. This may include:

  • IP address
  • browser type and version
  • device type
  • approximate geographic location (country or region)
  • pages visited and access timestamps

This data is used solely for site operation, analytics, and protection against abuse or fraud.

4. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or behavioral profiling. Third-party services used for hosting or payment processing may use essential cookies required for proper functionality.

5. Digital Products

We sell digital products only, including license keys and configuration files. Our products do not contain personal data, and we do not track how products are used after delivery.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Information may only be shared when necessary to process payments, comply with legal obligations, or protect against fraud, abuse, or security threats.

7. Data Retention

We do not maintain user accounts or customer databases. Any limited technical data collected automatically is retained only as long as necessary for operational, legal, or security purposes.

8. Children’s Privacy

This website is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the Website and prevent unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect immediately once posted on the Website.

11. Contact

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, contact us via Discord: oqe8. Support is provided through Discord messaging. Response times are not guaranteed.
